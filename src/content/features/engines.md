Most 15-year-old diesels have a history of deferred maintenance. These don't. The current owners brought both engines down to the frames: rebuilt alternators, replaced every hose and belt, swapped motor mounts, serviced heat exchangers, and repainted the engine rooms so any future leak is obvious at first glance.

- 2× Yanmar 3JH5E diesel engines, 40hp each
- All hoses and belts replaced
- Both alternators rebuilt (July 2023)
- Stainless steel motor mounts
- Heat exchangers serviced
- Engine rooms fully repainted — clean, inspection-ready
- 2× 80A Yanmar alternators + 2× 120A AMPLE alternators feeding the house bank
- 2× WS500 external regulators (new Oct 2021)
- 3× Racor 500FG fuel filters
- Approx. 2,800 engine hours (hour meters are known to be inaccurate)
- Last oil change: April 2024

The salon on a 27-foot beam is a lot of room, and this one is laid out to use every inch. A big dining table, lots of settee seating, a proper nav station with the electrical panels and electronics all in one place, and a separate desk we used for homeschooling and remote work — not a folding laptop corner.

The sliding glass doors out to the cockpit open very wide, and when they're open the salon and the cockpit become a single room. That, more than anything else, is what makes the boat live like a house.

- Wide salon with 6'3" headroom and panoramic views
- Big sliding glass doors integrate the salon with the cockpit
- 3-cabin Owner's Version — dedicated starboard suite with its own head
- 10-seat blue Sunbrella upholstered settee (Feb 2022)
- Big dining table
- 4× movable blue Sunbrella upholstered bar stools (Feb 2022)

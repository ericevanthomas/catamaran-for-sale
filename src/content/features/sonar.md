Traditional depth sounders only tell you what's directly under the hull — by the time a reef shows up, you've already hit it. Forward-looking sonar shows you what's ahead.

- Pantopix forward-looking sonar
- Side-scan sonar
- Installed February 2023
- Integrated with Garmin chartplotter display
- Raymarine ST60 tridata (depth, speed, temp)
- Raymarine ST60 wind instrument

Solar gives you a lot, but not at night and not in rain squalls. The D400 fills the gap. It's known for being one of the few wind generators you can actually sleep under.

- D400 wind generator — the quiet one
- Stop/run dump resistor (required with lithium banks)
- Charges the 1,100Ah LiFePO4 house bank
- Runs 24/7 — especially valuable in trade-wind anchorages

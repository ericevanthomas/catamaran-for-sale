Passive and active airflow was a priority in the build and subsequent upgrades. Every berth has a hatch above it; every cabin has a fan; every hull has keep-cool airflow even at anchor with no power.

- Large overhead opening hatches with Nicro solar ventilation fans
- 2× circulation fans in the master cabin
- 12V ventilation fans in both guest cabins
- Thermostat-controlled 12V cooling / ventilation fans in living spaces
- Thermostat-controlled engine-room ventilation fans in both hulls
- Overhead and bulkhead-mounted LED spot lights (white / red) for night ventilation without glare

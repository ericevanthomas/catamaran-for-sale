Designed for a family that works from the water. Five distinct workspaces mean multiple people can take calls, focus, or relax at the same time without treading on each other.

- Private office desk in the starboard owner's cabin — chair fits under the counter, with power and ventilation
- NAV / chart table below deck with full storage underneath, wired for 12V, 115V, and USB
- Main salon U-shaped settee with pedestal table — seats six
- Bar top with Corian work surface and stool seating for four — a natural laptop zone
- Outdoor cockpit table with cushioned seating under the bimini
- 12V and USB DC outlets in office area, NAV area, cockpit, and windlass locker
- Thermostat-controlled 12V cooling and ventilation fans throughout

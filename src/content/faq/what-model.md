question: What is a Voyage 500?
answer: The Voyage 500 is a 50-foot cruising catamaran designed by Simonis Voogd and built in South Africa by Voyage Catamarans, which has retired the 500 model but actively builds larger luxury cats. There is an active and supportive Voyage owner's group. Voyage yachts are known for their solid build quality, comfortable living spaces, and strong bluewater sailing performance. The owner's version, like Tropicalia, is popular with long-range cruisers and liveaboards. Voyage also made a 4-cabin version of the 500, which was popular in the Caribbean charter circuit. Voyage has an active luxury crewed charter base in the Virgin Islands.

A cruising catamaran needs two headsails: a big reaching/downwind genoa for light air, and a small self-tacking jib for upwind work or when the weather turns. This boat has both on furlers, so neither decision requires going forward.

- 130% furling genoa — 8.3oz High Modulus cross-cut Dacron, foam luff, UV cover
- Self-tacking furling jib (doubles as storm jib) — 8.3oz with UV cover
- Both on Profurl furlers
- Two reefing lines and luff pendants

The DC system has been substantially upgraded with lithium batteries, solar, wind generation, and modern battery management.

- LiFePO4 Lithium 1,300Ah 12v House Battery Bank (upgraded from 1,100Ah at purchase)
- 2 x 400W (800W total) Solar Panels Mounted on Bimini (2022)
- EPEVER Tracer421SBN MPPT 40 Amp Solar Regulator with MT50 Remote Meter (2022)
- 2x Engine Start Batteries 12V 65Ah
- House Bank / Engine Start batteries can be linked via Crossover Switch
- 2 x 120amp AMPLE Alternators with Smart Regulators (Rebuilt July 2023)
- 3 x Battery Isolator Switches
- Mastervolt Masterview Easy MK11 Battery Management System
- 4 x 200AMP BMS — Programmable w/Bluetooth (Oct 2021)
- 500Amp Over Volt Battery Protection Relay (Oct 2021)
- Mastershunt 500 12v Shunt and Mass Combi 100amp Battery Charger
- D400 12v Wind Generator
- Wind Generator Stop/Run Control Regulator and Dump Resistor
- Photocell Auto Anchor Light (On/Off Control)
- 12v Distribution Panel, Circuit Breakers, and Fuses

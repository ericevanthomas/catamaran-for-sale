Solar is the difference between anchoring out for a weekend and anchoring out for a month. This array is sized to cover daily house loads in full sun without touching the generator.

- 2× 400W solar panels (800W total) — bimini-mounted (2022)
- EPEVER Tracer 4215BN 40A MPPT charge controller
- MT50 remote display
- Feeds the 1,100Ah LiFePO4 house bank directly

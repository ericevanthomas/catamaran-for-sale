Autopilot failure is one of the most common offshore breakdowns. This boat has two complete systems wired in parallel. If the primary drive fails mid-passage, the backup is already there.

- 2× Raymarine ST7002 autopilot control heads
- WHITLOCK drive + Lewmar rotary drive — two independent actuators
- Raymarine X3 autopilot corepack
- Integrated with chartplotter, wind instruments, and AIS

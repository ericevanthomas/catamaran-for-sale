The mast has been pulled and stored on the hard every hurricane season for inspection. Any rigging components that needed replacement have been replaced as the rigger has called them out — so the standing rigging isn't all from one year, but nothing has been left in service longer than it should.

- Anodized aluminum triple-spreader mast
- 68'3" bridge clearance
- Harken mast hardware throughout
- Stainless wire standing rigging with swaged fittings
- Intermediate + cap shrouds
- Forestay and inner forestay
- Harken 5:1 mainsheet on a full-width curved track
- Spinlock rope clutches
- Masthead Windex
- Mast pulled and stored on the hard each hurricane season for inspection and maintenance

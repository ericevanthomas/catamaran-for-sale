The generator has been fully torn down and gone over — new hoses and a long list of new parts — and runs beautifully. With the lithium bank and 800W of solar doing most of the day-to-day work, we typically only fire the genset every few days to make water. When we do, it also powers the washing machine and keeps the bank topped up.

Start it from the owner's cabin or from the remote electronic panel at the nav station without ever leaving your seat.

- Onan 4kW diesel genset — fully torn down and serviced, new hoses and parts
- Approx. 4,615 hours
- Directly powers the Rainman watermaker and the washing machine
- Integrated into the Victron charging system
- Remote start/stop switch in the owner's cabin
- Remote electronic panel at the nav station

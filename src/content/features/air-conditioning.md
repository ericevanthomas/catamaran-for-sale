Two Mabru units, one on each voltage, so the boat is comfortable wherever you are. The 12V unit runs straight off the lithium bank—no inverter needed, no generator to fire up just to cool down. The 110V unit is for when you're on shore power or want to fire up the generator.

- Mabru 12,000 BTU, 12V DC, soft-start — runs off the 1,280Ah lithium bank. Master cabin and salon (May 2022)
- Mabru 17,000 BTU, 120V — port hull and salon. Runs on shore power or generator (June 2024)

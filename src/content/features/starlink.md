Starlink changed cruising. Passages that used to be weather-report-and-hope are now live-radar-and-GRIB. Anchorages that used to be sat-phone-only are now video-call-with-grandma. And for anyone with remote work, this one piece of equipment is the difference between a sabbatical and a lifestyle.

The boat has the motorized Starlink dish — the one with built-in actuators that tilt and rotate the antenna to keep a lock on the constellation regardless of how the boat's heading. It's mounted on the bimini and runs on 12V directly from the house bank, so it stays online without starting the generator. A brand-new Starlink Mini comes with the boat — still sealed in the box — as a backup or secondary unit.

The foredeck is where you'll spend half your time at anchor. Two big trampolines flanking a teak walkway make it both comfortable and safe to move forward underway.

- Offshore 3/8" polyester trampolines (Sunrise Yacht Products, 2017)
- Teak walkway between trampolines
- Integral chain guide down the centerline
- Teak aft platform extended by first owners for dinghy walk-past
- 11× 11" aluminum cleats + bollard throughout the deck

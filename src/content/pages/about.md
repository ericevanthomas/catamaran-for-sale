## Meet the family behind the boat

We are Eric, Alex, Leon, and Margot — a family of four based in Madison, Wisconsin. This is our second Caribbean cruise. The first was ten months aboard a monohull we called [*Nethermead*](https://www.sailingnethermead.com/), from Miami to Grenada and back in 2021–2022. The kids were small, we had a great time, and when we came home we knew we wanted to do it again. For the second trip we wanted more space, more comfort, and a little more safety for the kids, so we sold *Nethermead* ourselves and bought [*Tropicalia*](https://sailingtropicalia.com/).

Eric earned a USCG 100-ton Master captain's license and has crossed the Atlantic twice. Alex had never sailed before she met him. Leon is nine. Margot is six.

We sold *Nethermead* FSBO and it was straightforward. We're doing the same here — we have lived aboard, maintained every system, and are happy to walk you through anything and put you in touch with the people we've worked with on the upgrades.

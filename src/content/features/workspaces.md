Designed for a family that works from the water. We ran a business, homeschooled two kids, and held team meetings from this boat across a full Caribbean cruising season. Five distinct workspaces mean multiple people can take calls, focus, or just hang out at the same time without treading on each other — and each spot has power, Wi-Fi, and airflow.

- Private desk in the starboard owner's cabin — a real one, not a laptop corner. Power and ventilation at the seat.
- Nav / chart station in the salon with full storage underneath — wired for 12V, 115V, and USB. We used this for homeschooling every day.
- Main salon U-shaped settee with pedestal table — seats six
- Bar top with Corian work surface and four stools — a natural laptop zone with the galley right behind you
- Outdoor cockpit table with cushioned seating under the bimini
- 12V and USB outlets at every station — office, nav area, cockpit, windlass locker
- Thermostat-controlled 12V cooling and ventilation fans throughout

question: What is a Voyage 500?
answer: The Voyage 500 is a 50-foot cruising catamaran designed by Simonis Voogd and built in South Africa by [Voyage Yachts](https://www.voyageyachts.com/), which has retired the 500 model but actively builds larger luxury cats. There is an active and supportive [Voyage Catamaran Owners Worldwide](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1614218135459635/) Facebook group. Voyage yachts are known for their solid build quality, comfortable living spaces, and strong bluewater sailing performance. The owner's version, like Tropicalia, is popular with long-range cruisers and liveaboards. Voyage also made a 4-cabin version of the 500, which was popular in the Caribbean charter circuit. Voyage has an active luxury crewed charter base in the Virgin Islands.

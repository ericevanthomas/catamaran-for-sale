Solar is the difference between anchoring out for a weekend and anchoring out for a month. Where a lot of boats stick panels wherever they fit — and then watch the boom, the radar arch, or the mast throw shade across them half the day — these are positioned on the coach roof and bimini to stay in the sun. We routinely see close to rated output.

- 2× 400W solar panels (800W total) — coach roof and bimini mounted (2022)
- Positioned to minimize shading from the boom, mast, and arch
- EPEVER Tracer 4215BN 40A MPPT charge controller
- MT50 remote display
- Feeds the 1,300Ah LiFePO4 house bank directly

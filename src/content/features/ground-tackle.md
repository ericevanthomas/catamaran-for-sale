If you only upgrade one system on a cruising boat, it's the anchor. This one is already upgraded: oversized Rocna, heavy chain, and a windlass that eats it for breakfast.

- **Anchor**: Rocna 40kg (88 lb) — sized one step up for a 22,000-lb cat
- **Chain**: 200ft of 10mm G4 galvanized chain (new Nov 2019, re-galvanized Oct 2021)
- **Windlass**: Lofrans X3 12V 1700W — serviced 2021
- **Chain counter**: Quick QNC at the helm (2023)
- **Remote**: Windlass remote at helm
- 2× 3-strand nylon anchor bridles with stainless thimbles
- 2× chain hooks
- Floating anchor buoy, anchor tipper/guide
